Clubs

Director of Events

As the Director of Events for Management Information Systems Student Association (MISSA) at Cal Poly Pomona, I plan and host a number of enriching events throughout the academic year. These events include professional development workshops, networking sessions with industry professionals, and social gatherings between clubs across Cal Poly.

Annual Tech Mixer

Provides students with an opporunity to network face to face with industry professionals from MISSA's extensive alumni network. Students can learn about different career paths, gain insights into the tech industry, and make valuable connections that can help them in their future careers.

Cybersecurity & Awareness Fair

As a speaker at Cal Poly Pomona's Annual Cybersecurity and Awareness Fair, I bring to young students my knowledge and understanding of cybersecurity and business solutions. In 2024, the focus of our presentation was Social Engineering, with a live demo showcasing the dangers of open source intelligence and how to safeguard oneself from potential threats. This year, the focus is on cybersecurity concepts such as vishing, IP sniffing, and encryption, with a live demo featuring a mystery game and prizes.

Competitions

This past year, I had the opportunity to participiate in MISSA's Information Technology Competition and competed in the Data Analytics category. My team placed 3rd out of 9 teams, with students participating from various universities across the US.
